Le Jambons Most Inspirational Skateboarders

My top 3 Skateboarders of all-time

Mike Vallely

The first skateboarder i saw that had a different style and selection of tricks to all the others in the 2000's.
intoduced me to planting your feet as part of a trick.

Jason Adams

Pure style, making street skateboarding look like surfing, while everyone else was jumping down big stuff Jason was
skating fast and hard at walls and curbs.

Mark Gonzales

The Gonz has no right being at the bottom of any list. 'The Godfather of Street' should top all skateboarding lists,
anything The Gonz does looks good, inventing tricks in the 80's and 90's pioneering street skating alongside
Natas Kaupas he is a true legebnd in the sport.
